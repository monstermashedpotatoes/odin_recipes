Mac and Cheese

Description

Beautifully creamy and packed with protein, add your favourite hot sauce for an extra kick!

  • 330 grams pasta
  • 350 grams cottage cheese
  • 25 grams cheddar powder
  • 50 grams velveeta shreds
  • 10 grams nutritional yeast
  • 50 grams milk
  • 2 yellow bell peppers
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 1 pound 93% ground beef
  • 1 taco seasoning packet
  • Splash of water
  • 50 grams red enchilada sauce
  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add taco seasoning and a splash of water to the beef. Stir well and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the beef.
  5. Blend the cottage cheese, cheddar powder, velveeta shreds, nutritional yeast, and milk. Mix with beef and pasta
  6. Chop the bell peppers and onion, then add them to the skillet. Stir in the red enchilada sauce.
  7. Cook for an additional 5-10 minutes until everything is heated through and well combined.
